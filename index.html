Mr.Bean

Mr. Bean is a British sitcom created by Rowan Atkinson and Richard Curtis,
produced by Tiger Aspect and starring Atkinson as the title character.

Based on a character originally developed by Atkinson while he was studying for his master's degree at the University
of Oxford, the series centres on Mr. Bean, described by Atkinson as "a child in a grown man's body", as he solves various
problems presented by everyday tasks and often causes disruption in the process.
